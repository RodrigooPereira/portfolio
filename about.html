About Me

I'm a Portuguese Web Developer with a strong passion for building responsive and performant web applications that deliver great user experiences. With a keen eye for detail and a dedication to clean, efficient code, I strive to create digital solutions that are not only functional but also aesthetically pleasing. My expertise includes HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which I use to craft dynamic and engaging interfaces, as well as WordPress for developing versatile and user-friendly websites with custom themes and plugins that meet diverse client needs.

Outside of coding, I enjoy staying on the cutting edge of technology as a hardware enthusiast. I love tinkering with the latest gadgets and discovering how they enhance our digital experiences. This curiosity naturally extends to my passion for video games, where I appreciate getting lost in well-crafted game worlds, enjoying the stories and creativity that go into them.

Beyond the tech world, I'm deeply fascinated by Eastern religions, philosophies and cultures, which influence both my personal and professional life. As an avid Orientalist, I find great wisdom in exploring these ancient traditions, whether through books, meditation, or simply embracing their principles in my day-to-day life. This interest in mindfulness and balance provides a grounding contrast to the fast-paced tech world, complementing my passion for reading, where I dive into a wide range of genres that expand my understanding of the world. To maintain this balance, I also often retreat into nature exploring new landscapes or simply enjoying the outdoors allows me to recharge and maintain a sense of balance.
